Selected Work

Account Prioritization & Enablement Framework

A practical example of how I connect CRM structure, messaging, enablement, and process improvement.

Designing a scalable account prioritization and enablement framework

With more than 13,000 contacts in HubSpot, scoring and filtering helped, but they did not fully solve the day-to-day challenge of determining which accounts deserved the most attention, how often they should be worked, and which messaging path made sense.

The Challenge

Reps needed clearer prioritization and more confidence in technical, compliance-driven conversations. Training alone was not enough. They needed reusable messaging, practical value propositions, and structured decision support.

The Approach

I helped build an evolving framework that connected account tiering, compliance and vertical tagging, messaging organization, CRM visibility, and outreach workflows. It was refined over time as patterns became clearer.

Tier 1

Strategy: Higher-touch outreach, stronger personalization, ABM alignment

Tier 2

Focus: Strong operational fit with identifiable business need

Strategy: Structured outreach supported by reusable messaging paths

Tier 3

Focus: Lower-priority or lower-urgency accounts

Strategy: Lower-frequency nurture and general awareness messaging

Impact

  • Improved prioritization across a large contact database.
  • Reduced manual effort through reusable snippets, email templates, LinkedIn messaging, and call scripts.
  • Simplified compliance-related messaging for sales reps.
  • Increased consistency and confidence across outreach efforts.
  • Reduced time spent on onboarding, training, and repeated coaching.
